Gourmatic in Brief

The Crea Cubed engineers have created the first Turkish Gourmet vending machine in Turkey and the world, using hygienic and automated processes. The machine prepares fresh and hot toasts, wet burgers, pogacas, and simits without human touch in 90 seconds, thanks to AI software and double-layer packaging. The products comply with quality standards and offer three different cooking levels. The Gourmatic vending machine also serves cold and fresh Ayran, a traditional Turkish drink, in an automated way. It has a touch-sensitive order and advertisement panel, making it a unique vending machine.

The Gourmatic Vending Machines

Gourmatic, developed by our engineers, offers hygienic and hot toast within 90 seconds, along with beverage options. The machine is designed to deliver packaged products to customers quickly and under hygienic conditions, with food engineers overseeing the process. The toast is made with halal-certified ingredients, approved for quality and taste. Gourmatic features an interactive touch panel for easy ordering of multiple products, and its advanced technology allows for remote management of sales, prices, and product changes. The machine accepts standard payment methods, including cash, and requires less maintenance than other vending machines, with a minimized risk of failure.

The Smart Mobile App for Order and Payments

The "Smart Gourmatic" vending app - our ultimate solution for pre-ordering and paying for your favorite gourmet food and drinks from our Gourmatic vending machines!
